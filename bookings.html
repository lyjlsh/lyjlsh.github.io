bookings

tips on booking:

  1. browse the artist's instagram page for previous work and for details about booking and prices. available flash designs are also available on this website.
  2. contact artist and reserve an appointment according to the artist's booking system (usually done through emails when bookings open).
  3. wait to hear back from artist to confirm appointment time and dat.e
  4. pay the required deposit to confirm your appointment spot, prior to getting tattooed.
  5. artist will work with you on design prior to your appointment.
  6. show up at your appointment time to get tattooed!
